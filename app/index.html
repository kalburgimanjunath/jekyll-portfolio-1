Welcome

Recently graduated from Loughborough University with a 2:1 in Computer Science. Now working in London for MICROS eCommerce as a Front End Developer in the Studio team.

As is my way, I've made all of the website from the ground up, using only a couple of third-party libraries such as jQuery and Modernizr. A Little History

I have been making websites since I was about 13; steadily upgrading and improving as my skills progress. Up until late 2009, when I started at University, I was completely self-taught.

In 2007 I purchased my first domain (mstrutt.co.uk) and migrated all of my previously free-hosted sites onto subdomains of this. This move gave me a lot more freedom to progress and expand as I pleased, and today is still an excellent platform for my creativity.

While at University I worked freelance on projects for friends, family, and small businesses. Then in the summer of 2011 I did a year-long professional placement as a User Experience Developer with MICROS eCommerce, where I have since been offered a permanent position.
